Google Developer Student's Club (GDSC)

Google Developer Student Clubs at PCE organize and facilitate workshops, hackathons, speaker sessions, and study jams to provide students with technical development skills. By joining, you can attend workshops, webinars, hackathons, and other exciting events. Expand your technical skills through a peer-to-peer learning environment. Collaborate and apply the acquired skills by helping to build solutions for the local community. Participate in the annual Solution Challenge competition with the chance of winning prizes from Google.

Computer Society of India (CSI)

The CSI PHCET Student Club is PHCET’s student-led, community-first computer science and technology community where students interested in technology and latest industry trends come together to learn and grow in a engaging, open and welcoming environment. No matter what your current programming experience is or whether you’re planning on continuing in this industry or not, there will always be a place for you in our CSI PHCET Student Club We hope to see you soon as one of us as we go on to learn together.

Abhyudaya

ABHYUDAYA, meaning “Sunrise” in Sanskrit is a vibrant social and cultural club dedicated to celebrating the richness of cultures in India. This club promotes cultural awareness, meaningful connections, and personal growth and organize cultural events and social outreach program which help to showcase the club's positive contribution to society and culture. But this club is not just about having a good time, we're also passionate about making a positive impact by conducting social activities. Our mission is to promote social awareness and cultural diversity.

Hack Club

The Hack Club Orientation Event was organized with the aim of introducing the students to the Hack Club. The event was an hour-long and had over 150 attendees. The event started with a welcome speech by the organizer, followed by a presentation about the Hack Club, its objectives, and activities. The presentation also covered various aspects of technology, including programming languages, software development, and cybersecurity.

Artificial Intelligence & Data Science Association (AIDA)

The Department of Computer Engineering is going to start the Artificial Intelligence & Data Science Association(AIDA). The purpose of AIDA is to promote research, education, training and development in the realm of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, as well as subfields of AI like Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning, Data analytics, Business Analytics, Predictive Analytics.
