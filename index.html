Facilities

Studies at PHCET go beyond traditional classroom learning. We believe in a holistic approach to education that encompasses a wide range of experiences, including seminars and interactive sessions. Through these enriching experiences, we empower our students to become well-rounded individuals who are not only well-versed in their chosen disciplines but also equipped with the skills and confidence to thrive in the real world.

Curriculum Development

The department develops and updates the curriculum for its academic programs to stay current with advancements in the field. This includes designing courses, selecting textbooks, and integrating new technologies.

Teaching and Instructions

Faculty members in the department teach courses, lead labs, conduct tutorials, and assess students' progress through assignments, exams, and projects.

Research and Innovations

Faculty members and students collaborate on projects related to computer architecture, embedded systems, digital signal processing, robotics, artificial intelligence, and more.

Community Engagement

Computer Engineering department engages with the local community through outreach programs, coding camps, hackathons, and other initiatives to promote computer engineering and STEM education.

Seminars and Workshops

The department organizes seminars, workshops, and guest lectures by experts from academia and industry. These events provide students and faculty with exposure to the latest trends and developments in the field.

Student Support

Computer Department provide academic advising, career counseling, and support services to students. They also offer resources for job placement and internship opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are there opportunities for hands-on projects and practical learning?

    Yes, our curriculum emphasizes hands-on learning. Students work on projects, labs, and assignments that provide practical experience in designing and implementing computer systems.

  • What academic programs does the Computer Engineering department offer?

    Our department offers Bachelor's and Master's degree programs in Computer Engineering, providing comprehensive education in hardware, software, and systems.

  • Can students choose elective courses? What are some popular electives?

    Yes, students can choose elective courses based on their interests. Popular electives include Machine Learning, Digital Signal Processing, Mobile App Development, and VLSI Design
